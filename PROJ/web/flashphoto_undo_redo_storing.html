Undo Redo Buttons

The undo and redo buttons let you correct mistakes on the fly. The undo button lets you remove the most recent change you made to the image on the canvas, while redo lets you bring back the last set of actions completed.

Open and Save Buttons

The open and save buttons let you load images into flashphoto for editing with the open button, and then when you are ready they can be saved into a new file. File locations are easily selected through the menu in flashphoto. All images are saved as PNG files.
